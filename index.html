Our Story

How two

one.

Collin and Celine met on an ordinary Tuesday that neither of them would ever forget. A coffee shop, a borrowed pen, and one conversation that stretched long past closing time.

In the years that followed, they built a life rich with adventure, laughter, and a love that grew more certain with every passing season. Now, they invite the people they treasure most to witness what comes next.
