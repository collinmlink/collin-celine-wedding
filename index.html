Our Story

How two
worlds became
one.

Collin and Celine met on an ordinary Tuesday that neither of them would ever forget. A coffee shop, a borrowed pen, and one conversation that stretched long past closing time.

In the years that followed, they built a life rich with adventure, laughter, and a love that grew more certain with every passing season. Now, they invite the people they treasure most to witness what comes next.

"The best thing to hold onto in life is each other."
— Audrey Hepburn

Logistics & RSVPs

You can RSVP directly on this website using the form in the RSVP section. Please submit your response no later than January 3, 2027. If you have any trouble, don't hesitate to reach out.

Black Tie. We invite our guests to dress in formal attire — tuxedos and floor-length gowns are encouraged. Please avoid wearing white or ivory.

The ceremony begins at 4:00 PM. We kindly ask that guests arrive and be seated by 3:45 PM. Doors will open at 3:30 PM.

Street parking is available near the Annunciation Greek Orthodox Cathedral. Valet parking will be provided at La Colombe D'or for the reception — simply pull up to the front entrance on Montrose Boulevard.

The ceremony will be held indoors at the Annunciation Greek Orthodox Cathedral. The cocktail hour and reception will take place indoors at The Mansion House at La Colombe D'or.

We are not providing a formal shuttle service, but we encourage guests to use a rideshare service such as Uber or Lyft. Both venues are centrally located in Montrose and are easy to reach. We recommend arranging your return ride in advance.

We love your little ones — however, our wedding will be an adults-only celebration. We hope this advance notice allows you to make arrangements, and we look forward to celebrating with you.

Please reach out to our wedding coordinator rather than contacting us directly — we'll be a little busy! You can email us at hello@collinandceline.com and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

Accommodations

Yes — we have reserved a room block at a nearby hotel for out-of-town guests. Details and booking instructions will be shared here soon. In the meantime, the Montrose and Museum District neighborhoods offer a number of excellent options.

Most recommended hotels are within a 5–10 minute drive of both the ceremony and reception venues. Both are located in the heart of Montrose, making it easy to get around by rideshare.
