Our Story

How two
worlds became
one.

Collin and Celine met on an ordinary Tuesday that neither of them would ever forget. A coffee shop, a borrowed pen, and one conversation that stretched long past closing time.

In the years that followed, they built a life rich with adventure, laughter, and a love that grew more certain with every passing season. Now, they invite the people they treasure most to witness what comes next.

Our Recommendations

Boutique · Reception Venue
La Colombe D'or Hotel

A Houston landmark and our reception venue, La Colombe D'or is a beautifully restored 1920s mansion on Montrose Boulevard offering 32 luxurious suites. French cuisine, an infinity pool, and old-world elegance — and it is quite literally next door to the church.

3410 Montrose Boulevard, Houston, TX 77006

Boutique · Menil Collection

Opened in late 2024, this 71-room Bunkhouse property sits across from the Menil Collection in the heart of Montrose. Designed around the spirit of Houston — old world meets new — with a circular courtyard pool, lobby bar, and Aaron Bludorn's restaurant Perseid on site.

4110 Loretto Drive, Houston, TX 77006

The Marlene

A nine-room boutique inn housed in a restored 1910 neoclassical mansion in Montrose's Avondale Historic District. Individually designed rooms filled with hand-sourced French antiques, complimentary breakfast, and the on-site Bar Madonna. Intimate, romantic, and utterly unique.

Things to Do

Explore
Houston.

Houston's Montrose neighborhood is one of the most vibrant, walkable, and eclectic areas in the city. Whether you're here for a long weekend or just a day, there's something worth discovering at every turn. Click any spot to see it on the map.

01
Shopping · Dining · Lifestyle
Montrose Collective
An open-air collection of boutiques, restaurants, and coffee shops anchored by Uchi and a rotating cast of Houston's best local brands. A perfect afternoon stop — particularly on a weekend.
888 Westheimer Rd, Houston, TX 77006
02
Art Museum · Free Admission
The Menil Collection
One of the world's great private art museums, free and open to the public. The campus spans shaded oak lawns and several gallery buildings — including the ethereal Rothko Chapel nearby. A true Houston gem.
1533 Sul Ross St, Houston, TX 77006
03
Art Museum · Museum District
Museum of Fine Arts (MFAH)
One of the largest art museums in the United States, with a permanent collection spanning 6,000 years of history. Plan for at least half a day — and don't miss the rooftop for panoramic Houston views.
1001 Bissonnet St, Houston, TX 77005
04
Gardens · Hermann Park
McGovern Centennial Gardens
A stunning 5-acre garden in Hermann Park featuring a spectacular spiral walkway, seasonal blooms, and tranquil reflecting pools. Ideal for an early morning stroll or a peaceful afternoon escape.
1500 Hermann Dr, Houston, TX 77004
05
Golf · 18 Holes · Public
Hermann Park Golf Course
A classic 18-hole public course tucked inside Hermann Park, surrounded by the city's most beautiful green space. Affordable, accessible, and a Houston institution. Great for a casual round before the festivities.
2155 N MacGregor Way, Houston, TX 77004
06
Golf · PGA Tour Stop · Public
Memorial Park Golf Course
Home of the Houston Open, Memorial Park Golf Course is a meticulously maintained 18-hole championship course set against the backdrop of 1,500 acres of Memorial Park. One of the finest public courses in Texas — book well in advance.
1001 E Memorial Loop Dr, Houston, TX 77007
01
Tex-Mex · Casual
El Tiempo Cantina
A Houston institution. Sizzling fajitas, strong margaritas, and crab nachos that guests talk about for years. Classic Tex-Mex at its most unapologetic — perfect for a casual pre-wedding lunch with family.
322 Westheimer Rd, Houston, TX 77006
02
Steakhouse · Upscale
Drake's Hollywood
Montrose's most glamorous dining room. Dramatic interiors, an exceptional raw bar, and a menu centered on prime cuts and indulgent sides. The coldest martini in Houston and a silk pie worth every calorie.
1100 Westheimer Rd, Houston, TX 77006
03
Gulf Coast · All-Day Dining
Perseid
Chef Aaron Bludorn's Gulf Coast-inspired all-day restaurant at Hotel Saint Augustine. Quiet, refined, and quietly exceptional — the hanger steak and Anne potatoes are Houston's best-kept secret. Reserve ahead.
4110 Loretto Dr, Houston, TX 77006
04
Gastropub · Casual
Southern Yankee Crafthouse
Beloved Montrose gastropub with a creative cocktail program and some of the most talked-about pizzas in Houston. The pickle pizza with pepperoni is a genuine revelation. Great patio, even better vibes.
1312 W Alabama St, Houston, TX 77006
05
Texas BBQ · Counter Service
The Pit Room
Houston's finest Texas barbecue, full stop. The brisket has a bark and smoke ring that rivals anything in central Texas. Lines form early and sell-out is real — arrive before noon. Worth every minute of the wait.
1201 Richmond Ave, Houston, TX 77006
06
Seafood · Oyster Bar
Clark's Oyster Bar
A gleaming Montrose seafood counter with an impressive daily oyster selection, excellent chowder, and the most underrated burger in the neighborhood. Half-price martinis at happy hour. Elevated yet effortless.
3807 Montrose Blvd, Houston, TX 77006
01
Cocktail Bar · Hotel Bar
Bar No. 3
Tucked inside La Colombe D'or — our reception venue — Bar No. 3 is a moody, speakeasy-adjacent room of dark wood, soft leather, and live piano. Exceptional old fashioneds and a bartender, Sergio, who truly understands his craft.
3410 Montrose Blvd, Houston, TX 77006
02
Cocktail Bar · Late Night
Anvil
The gold standard of Houston craft cocktails, and one of the most respected bars in the country. Staff trained to an extraordinary level of precision. The "100 cocktails" list at happy hour is one of the city's greatest bargains.
1424 Westheimer Rd, Houston, TX 77006
03
Coffee · Wine · Open Late
Agora
A beloved Montrose institution open daily until 2 AM. Part coffee shop, part wine bar, part living room for the neighborhood. Worn-in couches, warm light, and a welcome lack of urgency. Perfect for a quiet afternoon or late-night wind-down.
1712 Westheimer Rd, Houston, TX 77098
04
Coffee · Breakfast
Blacksmith
Montrose's most beloved morning ritual. Exceptional espresso, housemade pastries, and a vibe that somehow feels both effortlessly cool and deeply welcoming. Arrive early on weekends — it fills up fast and for good reason.
1018 Westheimer Rd, Houston, TX 77006
05
Specialty Coffee · All Day
Siphon
Artisan-level specialty coffee in a warm, industrial space with excellent natural light and reliable Wi-Fi. A favorite of the neighborhood for focused mornings or long, unhurried afternoons. The cortado is exceptional.
701 W Alabama St, Houston, TX 77006
